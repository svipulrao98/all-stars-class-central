कुबेरनेट्स क्या है?

Kubernetes (also known as “K8s”) is an open-source container orchestration platform that was first developed by Google in 2014. Google had always used Docker containers to help squeeze every ounce of performance out of their servers to power intensive online services like Google Search and YouTube. To manage their rapidly growing number of clusters, Google ने बोर्ग (कुबेरनेट्स के पूर्ववर्ती) का निर्माण किया to automate the deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

बोर्ग ने एपीआई और उपकरणों का एक सेट प्रदान करके ऐसा किया जिससे डेवलपर को आपके अनुप्रयोगों की वांछित स्थिति को परिभाषित करने में मदद मिल सके।

Realizing the potential for this technology to become the industry-standard for managing containerized applications (and to indirectly monetize from it with their cloud services), Google released Kubernetes as एक स्वतंत्र और ओपन-सोर्स प्रोजेक्ट t under the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) to help speed up adoption and development. And it worked! Kubernetes is now the de-facto standard for container orchestration, with 2022 में 61% संगठन using Kubernetes to streamline the management of their containerized applications.

पिछले कुछ वर्षों में, कुबेरनेट्स का विकास और विस्तार जारी रहा है, बढ़ते उपयोगकर्ता आधार की जरूरतों को पूरा करने के लिए नई सुविधाओं और क्षमताओं को जोड़ा जा रहा है।

According to the स्टैक ओवरफ्लो डेवलपर सर्वे 2022 , Kubernetes is the second most loved and highly sought-after tool among developers and DevOps engineers. Additionally, the 10वीं वार्षिक ओपन सोर्स जॉब्स रिपोर्ट confirms that there is high demand for cloud and container technology as agreed by approximately 70% of hiring managers and IT experts. The salary reflects the demand, with कुबेरनेट्स इंजीनियर making an average of $150,000 a year according to ZipRecruiter, which is well above what the औसत सॉफ्टवेयर इंजीनियर बनाता है . Learning Kubernetes is essential for any DevOps engineer, so learn Kubernetes if you plan to become one!
