Ah, the good old days! Back when Class Central started covering भारी मात्रा में खुले ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम (MOOCs), all course certificates were free. Then, one by one, the major MOOC platforms began offering paid upgrades to verified certificates, then all but फ्री सर्टिफिकेट देना बंद कर दिया .

This isn’t to say that there isn’t still great content available for free. In fact, Class Central lists more than 1,600 कौरसेरा पाठ्यक्रम जो अभी भी पूरी तरह से निःशुल्क हैं . We’ve also catalogued close to 600 कोर्स आप मुफ्त में ले सकते हैं which are available through various course providers. These are essentially the same courses as those offered for credit in online degree programs.
